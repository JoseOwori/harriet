PHOTO PLACEHOLDER
================

This file is a placeholder indicating where your professional photo should go.

To add your photo:

1. Take a professional headshot or portrait photo
2. Resize/crop it to 500x500 pixels (square format)
3. Save as "photo.jpg" in this directory (replace this text file)
4. Refresh your browser to see the change

Recommended specifications:
- Format: JPG
- Size: 500x500 pixels
- File size: 50-100 KB
- Quality: Clear, professional headshot with good lighting
- Background: Plain or neutral

The website will automatically display it as a circular image in the header.
