Professional Summary

Assoc. Prof. Harriet Mutonyi is an experienced educator, researcher and consultant with over 15 years' experience across secondary and higher education. A former Dean of Education at Uganda Martyrs University and an INASP Associate, she specialises in curriculum and instructional design, qualitative research, capacity building for academics, and gender-responsive pedagogy.
